National Parks of the United States

Since Yellowstone became the first National Park in 1872, the United States has set aside lands for both conservation and public enjoyment. As of 2020, there are 62 parks, each with their own story. Explore the map and get to know them better!

Pan or search for a particular park, then click on each park outline to get more information. Turn on Points of Interest and Trails and hover over each location to get more information.

Use the slider bar at the bottom to see which parks existed during the time period selected.

Spatial data courtesy of the National Park Service. Some features digitized using official NPS maps. Points of Interest and Trail Rankings compiled with help of the National Park Service, Trip Advisor and All Trails. Created for UW-Madison, Geog. 575 by Greg Grube, Emily Johansen and Daniel Rau.
